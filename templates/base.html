About us

Sportshop.com is the online specialist for everybody who loves to sport, wants to feel fit or enjoys working out. Our large collection, specialist products and our excellent service are just a few things that are of huge importance for us. At the moment we specialize in field running, basketball and tennis.

The employees of Sportshop.com work out every day just for you. They are ready to answer all your difficult questions and they are happy to give you advice. When your order arrives we run as fast as we can to collect the items and ship them to you. Some of our colleagues are continuously looking to find the best products just for you!
